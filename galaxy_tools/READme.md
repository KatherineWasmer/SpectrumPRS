These are scripts used for my project that can be launched through Galaxy. 

1. Create an account at https://galaxy-main.usegalaxy.org/
2. Once you are logged in, click on the "Interactive Tools" tab on the top left corner of the page.
3. Choose the RStudio tool and upload any relevant files in your session
4. Wait for the session to start. It may take up to 5 minutes to load.
